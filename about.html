Tejomarg Infraprojects is a newly established company, structured from day one around the engineering, safety and quality standards that railways, power utilities and PSUs expect.

Who we are

A single, accountable EPC partner.

Tejomarg Infraprojects Private Limited is a turnkey EPC company focused on railway, transmission, signalling, underground and substation works across India.

As a new company, we combine the discipline of established engineering practice with the energy and responsiveness of a fresh team. Our model is deliberately simple: engineering, procurement and construction under one contract, so our clients deal with a single accountable partner rather than stitching packages together across many hands.

We are structured to prepare our own general arrangement drawings, bills of quantity and approval documents to RDSO, CEA and client standards — and to plan, procure and construct to programme with safety and quality built in, not bolted on. We are actively seeking our first mandates: tenders, sub-contract packages and joint-venture opportunities across our six disciplines.

Transmission corridor
The name

Tejomarg — the corridor of energy.

The name joins two ideas: tej, meaning energy or radiance, and marg, meaning path or corridor. Together they describe exactly what we build — the corridors along which power and people move: transmission lines, railway tracks, traction systems and the substations that tie them into the grid.

It is a name that sets the standard for the work: purposeful routes, engineered carefully, that carry energy where it needs to go.
