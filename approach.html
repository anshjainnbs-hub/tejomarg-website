Home / Approach

One accountable contract, from notice-to-proceed to handover.

Our delivery model is deliberately simple: engineering, procurement and construction under a single contract, with quality and safety planned in from the first drawing.

How a package moves

Four disciplined stages.

Each stage has clear deliverables, hold points and sign-offs — so progress is visible and defensible.

01

Engineering & Design

Surveys, detailed engineering, GADs, BOQs and approval drawings prepared to RDSO / CEA and client standards, with design review before release.

02

Procurement & Supply

Vendor development, technical scrutiny, stage inspection and logistics of conductors, towers, switchgear, rails and cable — quality assured at source.

03

Construction & Erection

Civil, foundations, erection, stringing, track and equipment installation to method statements and inspection & test plans, closely supervised.

04

Testing & Commissioning

Pre-commissioning checks, charging, integrated testing and statutory clearances — handed over with complete as-built documentation.

Coordinated, supervised works
Single-point responsibility

No interface gaps between packages.

On multi-discipline projects, the riskiest moments are the hand-offs — between design and procurement, between civil and electrical, between contractor and sub-contractor. By carrying engineering, procurement and construction under one contract, Tejomarg closes those gaps.
