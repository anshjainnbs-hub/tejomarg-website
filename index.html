Why Tejomarg

Single-point responsibility, from first drawing to first power.

Tejomarg means the corridor of energy — tej (energy) and marg (path). It is what we build: the lines, tracks and links that carry power and people across the country.

1
In-house engineering

Design cell prepares GADs, BOQs and approval drawings to RDSO / CEA standards.

2
Self-performed construction

Own plant and crews for foundations, towers, stringing, track and installation.

3
One accountable contract

Engineering, procurement and construction under one roof — no interface gaps.
